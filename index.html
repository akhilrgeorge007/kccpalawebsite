A Little About Us


Koottiyani Chacko Concerns Pala (KCC Pala), is committed to providing top-notch construction services tailored to our clients' unique needs. We specialize in a diverse range of areas, including architectural design, project planning, structural engineering, and construction management. Whether it's residential buildings, commercial complexes, industrial structures, or institutional facilities, we have the expertise to bring your vision to life.


What we do

Construction

We offer expertise in executing the entire construction process, from initial concept and feasibility studies to the final construction phase.

Interior works

We offer interior design services, helping clients with selecting finishes, materials, and creating aesthetically pleasing and functional spaces.

Renovation

We would assist in assessing the current structure, understanding the client's requirements, and developing a renovation plan.
